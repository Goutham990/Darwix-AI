Frequently Asked Questions

How often does GenAI sync my call data?

Darwix AI synchronizes automatically every 15 minutes to guarantee real-time score analytics.

Can I export the performance reports?

Yes, Administrators can trigger CSV & PDF batch exports from the main Dashboard metrics view.

How do I reset a team member's password?

Navigate to the Team Directory, open their Profile preview, and select 'Security Actions > Reset Credentials'.
